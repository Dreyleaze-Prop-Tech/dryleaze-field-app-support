Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check in to a job site?

Open the app, go to Open Jobs, select your job and tap Check In. The app will use your location to confirm you are on site.

How do I log a site visit?

Go to the Site Visits tab, tap the relevant visit, add your photos and notes, then mark it as complete.

Why does the app need my location?

The app uses your location to calculate your distance to job sites and to accurately record your travel and on-site time for payroll and job costing purposes.

How do I start my working day?

Tap the Start Day button on the home screen. Tap End Day when you finish. Your hours will be recorded automatically.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link.

The app is not loading. What should I do?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Close and reopen the app
  • If the issue persists, email us at info@dryleazebuilding.co.uk

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

1. Who We Are

Dryleaze Building and Maintenance Ltd ("we", "us", "our") operates the Dryleaze Field App. This app is provided exclusively to our employed field workers. Contact: info@dryleazebuilding.co.uk

2. What Data We Collect

  • Location data — your GPS location while the app is in use and while travelling to job sites, used to calculate distance to site and record on-site time.
  • Photos — images you capture or upload within the app for job site documentation and attendance records.
  • Time and attendance data — your start/end day times, check-in and check-out times at job sites.
  • Voice notes — audio recordings you choose to make within the app for site notes.
  • Account information — your name and email address used to log in to the app.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To record accurate working hours for payroll purposes
  • To document job site progress and materials
  • To calculate travel time and distance to job sites
  • To maintain records of site visits and completed work

4. Who We Share Data With

Your data is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. It is only shared with:

  • Dryleaze Building and Maintenance management for operational purposes
  • Our cloud infrastructure provider (Supabase) for secure data storage
  • Sentry, for crash reporting and error monitoring

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you are employed by Dryleaze Building and Maintenance, and for up to 6 years after employment ends in line with UK employment record requirements.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To make a request, contact us at info@dryleazebuilding.co.uk.

7. Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Access to the app requires authentication with a personal account.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes via the app or email.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related queries, email us at info@dryleazebuilding.co.uk.
